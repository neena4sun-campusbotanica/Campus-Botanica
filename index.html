Campus Botanica

Welcome to Campus Botanica, a botanical information portal dedicated to exploring and understanding ornamental plants found in gardens, landscapes, and educational campuses. Plants play an essential role in enhancing environmental quality, supporting biodiversity, and creating aesthetically pleasing surroundings. This website provides detailed information about four unique plants: Bougainvillea, Asparagus Fern, Triangle Palm, and Syngonium. By selecting any plant from the list below, users can learn about its scientific classification, identification features, growing conditions, benefits, common problems, propagation methods, care tips, and interesting facts. The aim of Campus Botanica is to promote plant awareness and encourage proper plant care among students, gardeners, and nature enthusiasts. Explore the collection and discover the fascinating world of plants.
